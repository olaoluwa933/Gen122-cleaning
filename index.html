About GEN122 Cleaning Services

Gen122 Cleaning has been providing High Quality cleaning service since 2009.

We are committed to providing the highest quality Residential and Commercial cleaning service by exceeding the expectations of our clients, as well as their employees and visitors to their premises or facilities through continuous improvements in Quality, Services and Productivity.

Our Mission

To maintain our breadth in range of specialisms by retaining staff knowledge & experience.

Investment in the training and development of staff, equipment and company growth to the benefit of all.

Maintaining our existing clients by maintaining excellence in our provision of services and encouraging referrals to new and potential clients through our quality services and price.

To be the Leading cleaning service within the industry by consistently providing high quality cleaning services.

Resource focus on maintaining cleaning standards with existing staff and company growth.

By constantly seeking out new contractors around the UK with whom we can work with and investing time and resources to ensure the contractors consistently work to our standards.

Our Vision

Through a commitment to excellence and continual investment in our people we seek to become the leading brand name within the cleaning industry nationwide. At Gen122 Cleaning, we strive to become part of your team. Partnering with us will provide you with access to a network of skilled cleaning operatives, who are cooperative, approachable, and committed to meeting your business’s exact needs.
